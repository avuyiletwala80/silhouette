## 🎯 3-Step Launch Process

### **Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages (5 minutes)**

This makes your website live on the internet.

**On your mobile phone or computer:**

1. Go to: https://github.com/avuyiletwala80/silhouette
2. Click "Settings" tab (top right, next to "Code")
3. In left sidebar, scroll down and click "Pages"
4. Under "Build and deployment":
   - Source: Select "Deploy from a branch"
   - Branch: Select "master"
   - Folder: Select "/ (root)"
5. Click "Save"
6. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
7. You'll see: "Your site is live at https://avuyiletwala80.github.io/silhouette"

**Your website is now live!** ✅

---

### **Step 2: Add Product Images (10-15 minutes)**

This makes your products look professional.

**What you need:**
- 12 JPG or PNG images of your fragrances
- Recommended size: 400x500 pixels
- File size: Under 500KB each

**How to upload:**

1. Go to: https://github.com/avuyiletwala80/silhouette
2. Click on "images" folder (or create it if it doesn't exist)
3. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
4. Select your 12 product images
5. Click "Commit changes"
6. Wait 2-3 minutes

**See detailed guide:** `ADD_PRODUCT_IMAGES.md`

- Wait 5 minutes for GitHub to update

- Check file names match HTML code
- Hard refresh browser
